# Request access for a new address

To request access with a different address, you have to follow these steps:

1. Click on the **‘’Connect Wallet’’** button on the landing page and select your preferred wallet option.
2. Under your addresses, click on the **‘’Request access with a different address’’** option.
3. Complete the form by providing the following details:
   - The REF# you wish to gain access to.
   - The address you would like to receive admin rights for.
   - A point of contact (e.g., X, Telegram, email).

> *Once you submit the request, we will contact you to verify your involvement with the specified REF#. Upon successful validation, the appropriate permissions will be granted.*
